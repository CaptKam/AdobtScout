🧭 Authentication Strategy for Scout
🎯 Core Principle

Never block emotional momentum.
Users should be able to browse, swipe, and fall in love with a dog before they’re asked to sign up.

🪄 Ideal Placement: After Emotional Hook
🔹 Recommended Flow:
Welcome → Intent → Lifestyle Quiz → AI Reveal → Swipe Discovery → ❤️ Like (x3) → AUTHENTICATE


Where to Authenticate:
➡️ Right after 3 “Likes” or when the user tries to message/contact a shelter or owner.

🔒 Moment 1: Post-Swipe Engagement (Primary)

After they’ve liked 3 dogs, trigger a modal:

“Save your matches so Scout can notify you when similar dogs appear near you 🐾”

CTA Buttons:

“Continue with Google”

“Continue with Email”

Why it works:

The user already has emotional buy-in.

Feels like a reward (saving matches) rather than a barrier.

Conversion rates at this point are typically 50–70% higher than pre-swipe signups.

🔒 Moment 2: Messaging (Secondary)

If a user taps “Contact Shelter” or “Adopt Me”, show:

“Create a free Scout account to message shelters and schedule visits.”

This feels functional, not forced — tied to the action’s purpose.

🔒 Moment 3: Optional Persistent Login (Tertiary)

At the top of the home page (hamburger or profile icon):

“Sign in to sync your favorites across devices.”

This is passive authentication, for users who proactively want to save data early.

💬 Psychological Framing
Timing	Emotion	Message Tone
Early	Curiosity	“Let’s explore together!”
Mid	Excitement	“Save your favorites!”
Late	Commitment	“Adopt your new best friend!”

Scout should ask for authentication only when users perceive value from doing so.

🔐 Technical Notes

Use Replit Auth (Google, GitHub, Email) for simplicity.

Store session tokens locally; users can remain “guest” mode until signup.

When they convert to authenticated, merge their local preferences + AI profile (no data loss).

Shelters and individual owners authenticate upfront, since they’ll upload/manage data.

✅ Summary
User Type	Auth Timing	Reason
Adopters	After emotional engagement (3+ likes or first message)	Maximize conversion & reduce dropoff
Shelters	Required at signup	Trust, moderation, and data control
Owners (Rehomers)	Required before posting	Accountability & authenticity

Final placement recommendation:

Add authentication after step 11 (“AI Reveal”) → during the first emotional action (like, save, or contact).
Never before the user swipes or discovers dogs.
