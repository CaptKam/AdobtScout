Surface area is too wide
Too many top-level routes and entry points for daily work.

If you fix those three, this becomes a calm, best-in-class system.

3. Pipeline: You Are Right, and This Is Fixable
Key Insight

Your pipeline is doing three jobs at once:

State

Validation

Documentation

That creates friction.

Fix the Stage Model
Merge These

❌ Ready for Adoption

❌ Listed / Available

Replace With

✅ Adoptable (Auto-Qualified)

Why:
“Ready” is a system state. “Listed” is a UI state. Users should not manage both.

Make Transitions Automatic
Auto-Transitions (No Dragging)

Intake → Medical Eval (on intake submit)

Medical Eval → Behavior (when medical cleared)

Behavior → Adoptable (when score complete)

Adoptable → Adoption Pending (on approval)

Pending → Adopted (on completion)

Manual Dragging Only For:

Exceptional cases

Overrides (Admin only)

Blockers Must Be Visible

Every blocked dog card needs:

🔴 WHY blocked

✅ What clears it

⏳ Who owns it

This is critical.
