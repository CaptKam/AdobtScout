🐾 SCOUT — Perfect Onboarding Flow & Process
⚙️ Overview

Goal: Make the user feel instantly emotionally connected and effortlessly guided — like a dating app but with empathy and purpose.
Duration: ~60–90 seconds total
Format: 11 micro-steps (1 question per screen, visual + swipe gestures)
Core Principles: Minimal friction, emotional resonance, AI personalization, immediate gratification

PHASE 1: EMOTIONAL ENTRY (Steps 1–2)

Purpose: Create instant emotional connection before data collection begins.

Step 1: Welcome Screen

Text:

“Meet your perfect companion. Scout helps you find a dog that truly fits your life — not just your lifestyle.”

UI:

Background: looping video of happy dog owners

CTA: “Start Matching” (primary), “Browse First” (secondary — no-login discovery)

Progress indicator (0%)

AI Behavior:

Begin a lightweight Replit AI session to cache early inputs

No login required yet — psychological safety (“no commitment yet”)

Step 2: Emotional Intent

Question:

“What brings you to Scout today?”

❤️ “I’m looking to adopt”

🏠 “I need to rehome my dog”

🐾 “I’m a shelter organization”

Purpose: Route user into the correct flow early (Adopter / Owner / Shelter).
AI Behavior: Branches and modifies tone + question order dynamically.

PHASE 2: ADOPTER LIFESTYLE DEEP DIVE (Steps 3–8)

Purpose: Build a soft psychological profile (AI compatibility model).

Each question = full-screen card with image, emoji, and swipe gestures.
Scout AI begins creating a “Lifestyle Signature Vector” behind the scenes.

Step 3: Living Situation

Question:

“Where do you live?”

Apartment 🏢

House with yard 🏡

Shared space / condo 🏙️

AI Note: Maps to dog size & exercise requirements.

Step 4: Activity Level

Question:

“How active are you on a typical day?”

Couch companion 🛋️

Weekend warrior 🚴‍♂️

Daily adventurer 🏃‍♀️

AI Note: Influences dog energy compatibility weighting.

Step 5: Work Schedule

Question:

“How long are you away from home most days?”

Rarely 🏠

4–6 hours

8+ hours

AI Note: Impacts independence, anxiety tolerance scoring.

Step 6: Household Composition

Question:

“Who lives with you?”

Just me

Partner / roommate

Kids 👶

Other pets 🐱

AI Note: Sets dog sociability preferences & child-friendliness filters.

Step 7: Dog Experience

Question:

“How experienced are you with dogs?”

New to this ❤️

Some experience

Experienced handler

AI Note: Adjusts complexity tolerance & training level fit.

Step 8: Personality Match

Question:

“What kind of personality are you drawn to?”

Calm & cuddly 💤

Playful & goofy 🎾

Confident & protective 🛡️

Shy but sweet 🌸

AI Note: Adds temperament affinity data to match vector.

PHASE 3: PERSONAL TOUCH (Steps 9–11)

Purpose: Humanize data capture & prepare emotional reveal.

Step 9: Aesthetic Preference

Question:

“Do you have a type?”

Small & adorable 🐶

Medium & balanced 🐕

Big & bold 🐕‍🦺

No preference ❤️

AI Note: Controls visual weighting in swipe feed.

Step 10: Commitment Readiness

Question:

“When are you hoping to adopt?”

As soon as possible

Within a few weeks

Just exploring for now

AI Note: Controls urgency and push notification timing.

Step 11: Compatibility Reveal

Loading screen: “Scout is learning your lifestyle... 🐾”

Then → Personalized confirmation screen:

“You’re most compatible with playful and active dogs who love adventure!
Ready to meet your matches?”

CTA:

“See My Matches” (→ swipe discovery)

“View Nearby Dogs” (→ map-based discovery)

AI Note:

Generates a short personalized paragraph using GPT-5:

“Scout thinks you’ll love Luna — she matches your outdoor spirit and relaxed evenings.”

Stores vector profile in local cache/session for future matching.

PHASE 4: SEAMLESS TRANSITION INTO EXPERIENCE
No Hard Signup Gate

Let the user swipe immediately — only prompt for account creation when:

They “like” 3+ dogs

Try to message a shelter or owner

Want to save matches

This mimics Tinder’s dopamine pattern while preserving low-friction onboarding.

Visual Flow Summary
Welcome → Intent → Lifestyle (6) → Personality (2) → Commitment → AI Reveal → Discovery


Each step < 8 seconds, 1 gesture, 1 visual.

PHASE 5: POST-ONBOARDING ENGAGEMENT
Scout AI Companion (GPT-5)

Once the user enters the discovery feed:

Scout AI introduces itself:

“Hi, I’m Scout 🐾. I’ll help you understand why each dog might be a great match.”

Explains compatibility scores (“Luna matches your energy level and schedule 92%”)

Learns from user swipes in real time (positive feedback loop)

💡 BONUS: SHELTER / OWNER ONBOARDING
Shelter Flow

Choose “Shelter”

Create Org Profile (name, city, contact)

Add multiple dogs (drag-drop upload photos, breed, age, personality tags)

Optional: “Let Scout auto-write descriptions using AI”

Preview on map

Owner Flow

Choose “Rehome”

Upload your dog’s info

Scout generates a heartfelt adoption story:

“Meet Bella, a gentle soul looking for a loving new home.”

Approve listing → auto-publish to local radius.

⚡ Final Conversion Triggers
Moment	Action	Outcome
After 3 Likes	Prompt: “Save your matches?”	Signup conversion
After First Chat	Prompt: “Enable notifications to get updates!”	Engagement
After 5 Swipes	Scout says: “Want me to show dogs near you?”	Enable location
After 24 Hours	Push: “New dogs that match your vibe 🐾”	Retention
🎨 Summary

Emotional Arc: Curiosity → Empathy → Joy → Discovery → Connection
Psychology: Frictionless flow, visual storytelling, instant gratification, personalized AI insights
AI Integration: GPT-5 vector matching + adaptive tone + behavioral learning
