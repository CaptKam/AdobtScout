✅ Product Design Needs Per Category
1. Adoption — Product Needs

Real-world need:
People want to find a dog that truly fits their life (not just looks cute).

Design Requirements

Replit should build:

Matching engine (lifestyle → dog personality)

Accurate dog profiles (age, energy, temperament, behaviors, training level)

Saved search alerts

Swipe / browsing UX

Chat or message system with shelters

Scheduling meet-and-greets

Fraud & repeat scammer protection

AI assistant to guide first-time adopters

Primary outcome Replit must deliver:
➡️ Reduce mismatch adoptions by ensuring compatibility.

2. Rehoming — Product Needs

Real-world need:
Owners need a safe, judgment-free way to find a better home when life changes.

Design Requirements

Replit should build:

Rehome onboarding flow (why, dog’s history, behaviors, triggers)

Identity verification (KYC-lite)

Safety screening to block backyard breeders or flippers

AI rewriting engine to help owners describe their dog honestly

Automatic matching with qualified adopters

Owner-to-adopter chat system

Optional anonymous mode

Primary outcome Replit must deliver:
➡️ Keep pets out of shelters by enabling safe, ethical rehoming directly.

3. Fostering — Product Needs

Real-world need:
Temporary homes are the pressure valve that prevents shelter overcrowding.

Design Requirements

Replit should build:

Emergency foster activation system
(hurricanes, fires, intake surges, hoarding cases)

Foster preference profiles
(size, behavior level, medical ability)

Short-term care timelines

Daily check-in system with reminders

Shelter → foster communication portal

Map of nearby animals needing foster now

AI instructions for medical/behavioral care

Primary outcome Replit must deliver:
➡️ Move animals into foster homes quickly to free shelter space and reduce euthanasia pressure.

4. Shelters — Product Needs

Real-world need:
Shelters need time, efficiency, and accuracy more than anything else.

Design Requirements

Replit should build:

Multi-user shelter dashboard
(volunteers, directors, intake coordinators)

Bulk upload of dogs

API importer for Petfinder / RescueGroups

Behavior notes system

Medical record tracker

Auto-posting to social & adoption sites

AI assistant to:

Write bios

Suggest matches

Screen adopter applications

Triage questions

Urgent animal alerts system

Primary outcome Replit must deliver:
➡️ Save shelters time so staff can focus on animals, not admin work.
