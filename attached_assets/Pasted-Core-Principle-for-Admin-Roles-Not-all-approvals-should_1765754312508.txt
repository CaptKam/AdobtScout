Core Principle for Admin Roles

Not all approvals should be handled by the same person.
Approvals differ in:

Risk to animals and people

Legal exposure

Reversibility

Volume and time sensitivity

Admin roles should map to those dimensions.

Recommended Primary Admin Roles
1. Platform Admin (Super Admin)

Purpose: Governance, overrides, policy enforcement

Approves / Controls

Shelter account approvals (final authority)

Foster account escalations

Edge-case application overrides

Policy exceptions

Account suspensions / bans

Characteristics

Small group (founders, head of ops)

Rarely involved in routine approvals

Has override power on all states

In Your Diagram

Final state authority on:

ShelterPath → Approved

FosterApproved (only if escalated)

AdminApplicationReview → Decision (override only)

2. Trust & Safety Admin (Primary Approval Workhorse)

Purpose: Risk evaluation and human verification

This is your most important role.

Approves

Foster applications (standard)

Adoption applications (final human decision)

Reviews AI + phone transcripts

Flags suspicious behavior

Rejects / Requests Info

Incomplete or inconsistent applications

Failed AI screening patterns

Phone screening concerns

In Your Diagram

Owns:

AdminFosterReview

AdminApplicationReview

Decision

Key Tools They Need

AI score + explanation

Transcript highlights (red/yellow/green)

Historical applicant behavior

Shelter notes

3. Shelter Admin (Organization-Level)

Purpose: Shelter-specific approvals and operations

Approves

Adoption applications for their own dogs

Meet & greet scheduling

Shelter-specific requirements

Cannot

Approve platform access

Override Trust & Safety rejections

Approve fosters globally

In Your Diagram

Participates after:

ApplicationApproved

Co-decision model:

Trust & Safety = “Eligible”

Shelter Admin = “Final Match Approval”

This mirrors Airbnb Host approval after platform vetting.

4. AI Operations / Review Admin (Optional but Powerful)

Purpose: Quality control for AI decisions (not users)

Does NOT approve users directly

Responsibilities

Review false positives / negatives

Tune AI scoring thresholds

Audit transcripts and outcomes

Monitor bias and drift

In Your Diagram

Observes:

AIReview

PhoneScreening

TranscriptReview

This role protects long-term system trust.

Optional Supporting Roles (Later Stage)
Support Admin

Handles user questions

Triggers “Needs More Info”

No approval authority

Compliance / Legal Admin (Future)

Incident review

Bite history

Liability events

Clean Role-to-Flow Mapping
Flow Stage	Primary Role
Shelter onboarding	Platform Admin
Foster onboarding	Trust & Safety Admin
AI screening	Automated
Phone screening	Automated
Transcript review	Trust & Safety Admin
Adoption approval	Trust & Safety → Shelter Admin
Overrides	Platform Admin
One Critical Structural Recommendation
Separate Eligibility from Match Approval

Trust & Safety answers:
“Is this person allowed to adopt/foster on the platform?”

Shelter Admin answers:
“Is this the right home for this specific dog?”

This separation dramatically reduces liability and admin fatigue.
